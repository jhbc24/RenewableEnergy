Renewable Energies

Solar Energy

Solar energy is the most abundant, renewable, and sustainable energy source. It's harnessed using solar panels and can be used to generate electricity, heat, and even power entire homes.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is captured using wind turbines and converted into electricity. It's a clean and renewable source of power that contributes to reducing carbon emissions.

Hydro Energy

Hydro energy is generated from the movement of water, typically using dams. It is a reliable and renewable energy source, particularly in regions with abundant water resources.
